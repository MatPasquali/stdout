### [LKValues: Aligning Large Language Models with Sri Lankan Societal Values](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20410v1)
`arXiv`

Researchers have introduced LKValues, a new approach to aligning large language models with Sri Lankan societal values, a crucial step in overcoming current cultural limitations, as existing language models tend to be biased towards Western norms. This is particularly relevant in multilingual societies such as Sri Lanka, where local values are unique and may be mishandled. LKValues was developed from a survey of 205 respondents, combining adapted global frameworks and local constructs elicited from language models, resulting in 40 majority-endorsed societal values.
