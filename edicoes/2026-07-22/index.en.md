### [OmniReasoner: Thinking with Long Audio-Video via Native Tool Use](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19339v1)
`arXiv`

Researchers have developed OmniReasoner, a post-training tool to help multimodal language models reason with long audio-visual content, overcoming current limitations due to the sparsity and cross-modal nature of crucial evidence. OmniReasoner works by building a low-cost global preview of the full stream and, when needed, calling a zoom-in tool for more detailed inspection of specific temporal intervals. This allows models to observe different sampling granularities before and after the call, improving decision-making capabilities. This approach may revolutionize how language models process multimodal information, by enabling more efficient and effective use of available data.
