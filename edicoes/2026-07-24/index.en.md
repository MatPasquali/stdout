### [Graph Learning on Ensembles of Cyclic Peptides: An Investigation of Molecular Ensemble Modeling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.21561v1)

The EnsembleEGNN, a graph-based learning model, uses shared equivariant graph neural network layers to encode each conformer, and a set attention block to combine the resulting representations.
